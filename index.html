Meet The developers

The founders of BookMyShow are 3 friends, Ashish Hemrajani, Parikshit Dar, and Rajesh Balpande. They are the alumnus of Sydenham Institute of Management, Mumbai University. Ashish Hemrajani is the CEO of BookMyShow.

Ashish Hemrajani

Ashish Hemrajani is the CEO and Co-founder of Bigtree Entertainment Private Limited, which operates BookMyShow. After completing his MBA from Sydenham University in 1997, he gave in to his passion for entrepreneurship, and founded Bigtree Entertainment Pvt. Ltd in 1999.

Parikshit Dar

Parikshit Dar and Rajesh Balpande studied at Sydenham College of Commerce and Economics. While Rajesh was an investment banker with the Chatterjee Group earlier, Parikshit began his career working with the Dutch Multinational retailer SPAR. All the three of them got to meet each other during their stint at Sydenham.

Rajesh Balpande

Rajesh Balpande is a Co-founder of BookMyShow, where he serves as a Board Member. He is also an Angel Investor. He started his career in 1997 as an Investment Banker at Chatterjee Soros Group. Post this stint, he helped set up Big Tree Entertainment in 1999.
